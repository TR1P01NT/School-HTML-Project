MacBook Air

Light.Speed.


The New MacBook Air now features a brilliant Retina display, new Magic Keyboard, Touch ID, processors with up to twice the performance, faster graphics, double the storage capacity and all-day battery life.

Retina Display

With a resolution of 2560x1600 for over 4 million pixels, images take on a new level of detail and realism and text is sharp and clear. True Tone technology automatically adjusts the white point of the display to match the colour temperature of your environment.

Performance

MacBook Air can packs up to quad-core Intel Core i7 processor, 16GB of high-performance 3733MHz memory and up to 2TB of fast SSD storage into just 1.29kg. It can delivers up to two times faster CPU performance, 80 percent faster graphics performance, lets you work seamlessly even with multiple apps open and also providing plenty of room for all your movies, music, photos, files and games.

Keyboard and Trackpad

MacBook Air now features the new Magic Keyboard, first seen on the 16‑inch MacBook Pro.The refined scissor mechanism delivers a responsive, comfortable and quiet typing experience and the backlit keys with ambient light sensor help you type in low-light settings. The Force Touch trackpad lets you interact with your Mac in a variety of ways based on subtle differences in pressure no matter where you click the trackpad.

Touch ID

The advanced security and convenience of Touch ID are built into MacBook Air. Simply place your finger on the Touch ID sensor and your Mac unlocks. You can also use your fingerprint to immediately access locked documents, notes and system settings, without entering yet another password. MacBook Air features the Apple T2 Security Chip designed by Apple to make MacBook Air even more secure. All your information stays safe and all your data stored on the SSD is automatically and fully encrypted.

Speakers and Camera

The new stereo speakers deliver twice the bass and 25 percent more volume. Wider stereo sound creates immersive audio when you’re listening to music or watching a movie.The FaceTime camera lets your friends and family members can see you in high definition and the three microphones form an array that more accurately captures your voice for FaceTime calls and dictation.

Thunderbolt 3

Thunderbolt 3 combines ultra-high bandwidth with the ultra-versatility of the USB-C industry standard to create one revved-up universal port. Thunderbolt 3 integrates data transfer, charging and video output in a single connector, delivering up to 40 Gbps of throughput for twice the bandwidth of Thunderbolt 2 and Thunderbolt 3 is reversible, so no matter how you plug it in, it’s always the right side up.

Materials

MacBook Air is made with an aluminium alloy created by Apple that delivers the same strength, durability and flawless finish — without mining any new aluminium from the earth.
